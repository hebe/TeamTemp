A quick, anonymous temperature check for your team.
No tracking. No judgments. Just honest signals.

How it works

1 Your team admin creates a round and shares a link — in Slack, email, wherever.
2 Everyone takes 30 seconds to answer a few simple questions. Totally anonymous.
3 The team dashboard shows patterns and changes over time — things worth talking about together.

TX Temp is a shared thermometer on the wall — not a report card.
